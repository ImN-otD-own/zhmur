Huguette Caland

Huguette Caland (arabic: أوغيت الخوري) b. 1931) is an abstract artist and fashion designer from Beirut, who was born into a family of Lebanese politicians in 1931. The only daughter of the first President of Lebanon, Bishara El-Khoury, who served in this post for 9 years (1943-1952).
